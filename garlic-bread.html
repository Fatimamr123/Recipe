Garlic Bread

Buttery, garlicky, and crispy bread that's perfect as a side dish or appetizer.

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 4–6

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1 loaf French or Italian bread
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp dried oregano (optional)

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Slice the loaf of bread in half lengthwise.
  2. In a bowl, combine softened butter, garlic, parsley, salt, pepper, and oregano (if using).
  3. Spread the garlic butter mixture generously on both halves of the bread.
  4. Place the bread on a baking sheet and bake for 10–12 minutes, until golden and crispy on the edges.
  5. Slice and serve warm. Enjoy!
