Chickpea Salad

A simple, fresh salad with chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, and a tangy lemon dressing. Perfect for lunch or as a side dish!

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 2–4

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, and parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, honey (if using), salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss everything together gently.
  4. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 30 minutes to let the flavors meld.
