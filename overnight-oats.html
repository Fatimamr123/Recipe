Overnight Oats

A nutritious, make-ahead breakfast packed with fiber and customizable flavors.

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 1

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk or almond milk
  • 1/4 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  • Fruit and nuts for topping

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. Combine oats, milk, yogurt, honey, and cinnamon in a jar or container.
  2. Mix well and seal. Refrigerate overnight (at least 6 hours).
  3. Top with fruits, nuts, or seeds before serving. Enjoy cold!
