Roasted Veggie Bowl

A colorful, hearty bowl full of roasted seasonal vegetables, grains, and a tahini drizzle.

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 2

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato, cubed
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or rice
  • Tahini or hummus, for drizzling

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss veggies with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread on a baking sheet.
  3. Roast for 25–30 minutes until golden and tender.
  4. Serve over quinoa or rice. Drizzle with tahini or add a scoop of hummus.
