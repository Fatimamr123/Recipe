Spaghetti Carbonara

A traditional Roman pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper — simple and delicious!

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 2–3

🧾 Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 garlic clove, peeled
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt, to taste

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water according to the package instructions. Reserve 1/4 cup pasta water before draining.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta with garlic until crispy. Remove garlic.
  3. Whisk eggs and Parmesan together in a bowl. Add lots of black pepper.
  4. Mix drained pasta with pancetta (off heat), then quickly stir in the egg mixture. Add a little reserved water to make it creamy.
  5. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top.
