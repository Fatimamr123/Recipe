Caprese Salad

A fresh and vibrant Italian salad made with ripe tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil, drizzled with olive oil.

🍽️ Yield:

Serves 2–4

🧾 Ingredients

  • 2 large ripe tomatoes
  • 200g fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Balsamic glaze (optional)

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. Slice tomatoes and mozzarella into even, round slices.
  2. Arrange them alternately on a plate with basil leaves in between.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add a drizzle of balsamic glaze if desired. Serve immediately.
