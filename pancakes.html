Classic Pancakes

Fluffy, light, and perfect for breakfast — these pancakes are a weekend favorite!

🍽️ Yield:

Makes about 6 pancakes

🧾 Ingredients

  • 1 cup (120g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup milk (180ml)
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • Butter or oil for cooking

👩‍🍳 Preparation

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk egg, milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed (don’t overmix).
  4. Heat a nonstick pan over medium heat. Add a bit of butter or oil.
  5. Pour ¼ cup batter per pancake. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook other side.
  6. Serve hot with syrup, fruits, or your favorite toppings.
